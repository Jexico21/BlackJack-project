In BlackJack, you start with 2 random cards and your goal is to reach a point value of 21, a blackjack, or get a higher score than your opponents.
To draw another card, you "hit" but to not receive another card you "stay". If you get over 21, you "bust" and lose. If you draw 5 cards without busting, you automatically win unless the dealer has a blackjack.
Any "A"/"Ace" cards are worth 1 or 11, you get to pick. The cards are worth the number on them and if they are face cards, their value is 10
